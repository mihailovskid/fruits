Orange

Oranges and orange juice The orange is the fruit of the citrus species Citrus x sinensis in the family Rutaceae.[1] It is also called sweet orange, to distinguish it from the related Citrus x aurantium, referred to as bitter orange. The sweet orange reproduces asexually (apomixis through nucellar embryony); varieties of sweet orange arise through mutations.
